About our school

A school that feels like home — only with more wonder.

GrowingKids was founded on a simple belief: every child carries a spark. Our pre-primary and middle school program is designed to keep that spark alive through joyful exploration, strong academic foundations, and genuine care.

From the principal's desk

"A child's first school should feel like a warm hug."

At GrowingKids, we don't just teach lessons — we listen, observe and walk alongside each child. Our teachers are trained to notice the quiet wonder in every classroom and to turn it into a moment of learning. We believe academic strength and emotional well-being grow side by side.

When you choose our school, you're choosing a community that celebrates curiosity, kindness and courage in equal measure.

Mrs. Anjali Sharma
